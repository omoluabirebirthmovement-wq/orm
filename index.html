About Omoluabi Rebirth Movement

A structured response to character decay and the illusions of fast money.

The Omoluabi Manifesto

We believe that character is wealth (Iwa l'ewa). We declare that honor is far greater than fast money acquired through deception. We reject cultism, ritualism, and drugs. We embrace intellectual diligence, digital creativity, and respect for humanity. We are builders of the future. We are Omoluabis.

Our Mission

To reconstruct the moral fabric of African youths by restoring integrity, discipline, entrepreneurship, and leadership, while directly combating internet fraud, cultism, and drug abuse.

Our Vision

A new generation of proud African teenagers and youths recognized globally for their high integrity, creative genius, robust leadership, and legal prosperity.

Core Values Grid

The standard definitions governing members, leaders, and ambassadors.

Integrity

Holding absolute honor in words and deeds.

Discipline

Exercising self-control over destructive temptations.

Leadership

Steering communities towards positive development.

Respect

Honoring parents, community guidelines, and peers.

Purpose

Chasing meaningful long-term goals instead of shortcuts.

Creativity

Utilizing intellect to construct legitimate tools.

Excellence

Executing tasks and legitimate skills flawlessly.

Character Code

Upholding the Omoluabi creed at all times.

Founder's Message

Our continent's future depends entirely on the character of our youth. The Omoluabi Rebirth Movement was founded not just to say 'No' to internet fraud and social vices, but to provide a cinematic, emotional, and robust alternative. We give youths a positive identity, teach them high-income legitimate skills, and support them when they choose to step away from dark lifestyles. Join us in rebuilding the foundations.

Engr. Jimoh Lawal Akinlabi (Akinkanjuoyo)
Founder & Visionary Leader, Omoluabi Rebirth Movement
